Account & Data Deletion Policy

App Name

Cosmo

Developer

Vikash NITK

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly from the Cosmo app:

  1. Open the Cosmo app.
  2. Go to Account Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account and confirm the action.

This will permanently delete your account and all associated data.

What Data is Deleted

  • Your account information (name, email, profile picture)
  • Your preferences and settings
  • All in-app activity and history

Data Retention

Some data (such as transaction records) may be retained for up to 30 days to meet legal or financial obligations.

Need Help?

If you are unable to access the app, you may email us at support@cosmoapp.com to request deletion.
